Matt Wallingford

I am a research scientist on the PRIOR team at the Allen Institute for AI (AI2). I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Washington, where I was advised by Ali Farhadi.

Before Seattle, I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Physics and an M.S. in Computer Science from Cornell University, where I was advised by Bharath Hariharan.
